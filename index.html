Consulting for a VC Firm

Gave firm a model to analyze future investments.

Through MECC Consulting, my team and I improved a venture capitals firm’s deal criteria matrix using several different computer models - decision tree, neural networks and a regression tree among others – in order to predict the success of an investment. I personally developed and implemented a GUI in Python for a desktop app that has options to train and test our model, which is not only still in use today by the company, but is also continuously updated with more investment data. Additionally, we analyzed and interpreted past investment data for the firm using excel and radiant to identify trends within successful and failed companies, which provided foresight into distinguishing qualities among potential investments. Ultimately, we found factors that the firm was using that actually negatively correlated to success - most important being market size.

  • Date: January - May 2019
  • Client: Venture Capital Firm
  • Skills Used: Machine Learning , Python (PyQT5, NumPy)
  • Final Deliverable: Desktop App that predicts investment success based on factors scored by VC team

Anonymouse App

Fought censorship with technology

My team and I developed an Android microblogging app, called Anonymouse, that uses Bluetooth LE scan to incorporate a novel infrastructure-less communication technology to prevent censorship for college students in mainland China. We also implemented a user-interface for the app that displays only relevant posts based on degrees of separation. We did beta testing on the UMich campus and are hoping to have the app production ready later this year.

  • Date: January 2019 - January 2020
  • Skills Used: Android Studio, Java
